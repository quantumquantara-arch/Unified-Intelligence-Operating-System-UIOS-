# UIOS Interstellar Communications & Navigation

## Overview
The Interstellar Communications & Navigation module (ICN) is UIOS’s long-range intelligence layer designed for:
- Starship deep-space missions  
- Lunar and Martian comms networks  
- Autonomous interplanetary navigation  
- Fleet synchronization across extreme distances  
- Ultra-low latency routing via Starlink’s evolving constellation  

ICN ensures that every mission — Earth orbit, Moon, Mars, or deep-space — remains connected, stable, safe, and precisely navigated.
