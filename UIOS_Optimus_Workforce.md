The Optimus Workforce Intelligence Module is the central coordination layer for all Optimus humanoid robots operating across Tesla factories, SpaceX launch sites, Starlink deployment teams, Neuralink clean rooms, Tesla Energy installations, and future Mars and lunar construction projects.
