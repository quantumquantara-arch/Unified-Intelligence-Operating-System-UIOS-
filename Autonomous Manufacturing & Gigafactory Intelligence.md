# Autonomous Manufacturing & Gigafactory Intelligence
A unified autonomy system enabling Tesla Gigafactories to operate as self-optimizing, self-adapting, and self-repairing production organisms using UIOS, Optimus, Starlink, predictive analytics, and real-time simulation.

---

## 1. Purpose

To transform Gigafactories into:
- self-coordinating manufacturing systems  
- predictive maintenance environments  
- fully automated material-flow engines  
- real-time adaptive production lines  
- zero-downtime, 24/7 autonomous factories  

This is the manufacturing layer of a planetary-scale AI-powered production ecosystem.
