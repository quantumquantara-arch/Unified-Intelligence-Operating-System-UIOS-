The UIOS Global Defense & Threat-Mitigation System forms the planetary-scale safety shield that protects all Musk-ecosystem infrastructure — and eventually entire cities, nations, orbital platforms, and Mars habitats — from physical, digital, environmental, systemic, and emergent risks.  

It provides a unified, fully autonomous, always-on surveillance, prediction, and interception framework built on coherence, temporal foresight, and multi-domain intelligence.
