# UIOS Manufacturing Intelligence

## Overview
The Manufacturing Intelligence System (MIS) is UIOS’s factory brain —  
a full-stack, self-optimizing, predictive manufacturing engine that runs:
- Gigafactories  
- Optimus assembly  
- Dojo hardware lines  
- Battery cell production  
- Robotics fabrication  
- Starship component machining  
- Energy system manufacturing  
- Warehouse and logistics centers  

MIS turns a factory into a coherent living organism that anticipates problems, eliminates waste, maximizes throughput, and protects humans.

---

## 1. Factory-Wide Coherence Engine

### 1.1 Unified Factory Graph
Every machine, robot, sensor, conveyor, and workstation is represented as a node inside a global coherence map with:
- real-time status  
- energy use  
- health signals  
- queue depth  
- task state  
- predicted risk  
- optimal next action  

This allows the entire factory to self-balance.

---

### 1.2 Flow Harmonization
MIS continuously adjusts:
- material routing  
- robot pacing  
- human-robot interactions  
- battery charging cycles  
- temperature-sensitive steps  
- station load distribution  

Flow remains smooth, coherent, and bottleneck-free.
