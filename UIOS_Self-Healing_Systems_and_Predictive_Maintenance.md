# UIOS Self-Healing Systems & Predictive Maintenance Engine

## Overview
The Self-Healing & Predictive Maintenance Engine (SHPME) enables UIOS-powered systems  
(Tesla vehicles, Starship, Optimus, Gigafactories, Starlink, energy grids)  
to detect, predict, and repair faults before failure occurs.

It massively reduces downtime, increases lifespan, and supports planetary-scale operations.
