# UIOS Planetary Economy Optimizer

## Overview
The Planetary Economy Optimizer (PEO) is UIOS’s large-scale coordination engine  
that balances the global flow of:

- resources  
- labour (human + Optimus)  
- energy  
- supply chains  
- manufacturing loads  
- transportation networks  
- Starlink connectivity  
- demand signals  
- market volatility  
- environmental constraints  

PEO transforms the world into a coherent, self-stabilizing economic organism —  
one where scarcity collapses, resilience skyrockets, and operational waste approaches zero.
