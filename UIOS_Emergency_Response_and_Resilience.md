# UIOS Emergency Response & Resilience System

## Overview
The Emergency Response & Resilience (ERR) module is UIOS’s planetary crisis-stabilization engine.  
It unifies Tesla, SpaceX, Starlink, Optimus, Tesla Energy, and autonomous fleets into a single rapid-response intelligence layer that:

- detects emergencies early  
- prevents cascading failures  
- deploys the right assets instantly  
- stabilizes regions under stress  
- keeps humans safe  
- restores normal operations fast  

ERR is the backbone of safety for a multi-planet civilization.
